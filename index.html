Fertilizers

Fertilizers are products that are required for the enhancement of soil and retention of water for the growth of the plants.It supplies nutrients such as nitrogen, potassium and phosphorus to the plants for their effective growth.Fertilizers are available in different concentrations and formulations for different crops.Fertilizers are used in small gardens and acres of agricultural land. Different types of fertilizers are used depending on the soil and weather conditions. With the drastic increase in population, there has been the need of increased production of crops to provide food and other agricultural products.

Door Delivery

Door-to-door delivery service is a somewhat confusing term in the logistics industry. Ideally, it means the delivery of products from the seller’s warehouse to the point of delivery, i.e., to the end customer – the seller’s door to the consumer’s door.But door-to-door delivery also stands for the delivery of freight from the seller’s pick-up location to the consignee’s warehouse or transportation hub, from where it is transported to the customer’s doorstep. In either case, a door-to-door courier service does not require you to lift a finger to collaborate with different agencies to get your products delivered.

Stock Exchange

The Stock exchange is an organization that provides a market place where investigators may buy and sell shares of a wide range of companies.It is a market where sales and purchase of securities take place.Agriculture enterprises can raise money by offering shares for sale to the general public in the stock exchange.Agriculture enterprises operating under the stock exchange usually have more improved management standards and efficiency records than privately owned enterprise.

Cold Storage

A cold storage unit incorporates a refrigeration system to maintain the desired room environment for the commodities to be stored. A refrigeration system works on two principles: VAS, although comparatively costlier, is quite economical in operation and adequately compensates the higher initial investment.Storage and shelf life depends on intrinsic characteristic of items stored as well as the storage conditions. In general, the optimum storage temperature is as low as possible but still above the level of chilling sensitivity of the respective crop. The cold storage facilities now available are mostly for a single commodity like potato, orange, apple, grapes, pomegranates, flowers, etc. which results in poor capacity utilization.
